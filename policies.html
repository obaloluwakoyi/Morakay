Store Policies

Transparency and quality assurance for our valued customers.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 10:00 AM within Lagos. Orders placed after this time will be scheduled for the following business day.

  • Mainland: ₦2,500 — ₦4,000
  • Island: ₦4,500 — ₦6,500
  • Cold Chain: All products are transported in insulated coolers to maintain freshness.

Returns & Refunds

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns once the delivery has been accepted. However, your satisfaction is our priority:

  • If you notice any quality issues upon delivery, please reject the item immediately.
  • Report any discrepancies within 2 hours of delivery for a replacement or store credit.

Privacy Policy

Your data is secure with us. We only collect your name, address, and phone number to facilitate delivery and improve your shopping experience.
